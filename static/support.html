‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Why isn't the analysis working?

A: Make sure the video has captions/subtitles available. WorthIt.AI works by analyzing the video's transcript and comments. If a video doesn't have captions, we can't analyze it.

Q: How accurate is the Worth-It score?

A: The score is based on AI analysis of content depth, comment sentiment, and overall video quality. It's a helpful guide, but use your judgment too!

Q: Does WorthIt.AI work with all YouTube videos?

A: Most videos work, but some may not if they don't have captions, are age-restricted, or have disabled comments.

Q: Is my data private?

A: Yes! We only analyze public video data (transcripts and comments). We don't collect personal information or track your activity.

Q: Why does the analysis take time?

A: We're processing video transcripts and comments with AI to give you meaningful insights. This takes a few seconds but provides much better results than simple summaries.

üîß Troubleshooting

If the app won't analyze a video:

  • Check your internet connection
  • Make sure the video has captions/subtitles
  • Try a different video to test
  • Restart the app if needed

If the Share Extension doesn't appear:

  • Make sure WorthIt.AI is installed
  • Try sharing from the YouTube app
  • Check that you're sharing a YouTube URL

If the analysis is slow:

  • Longer videos take more time to analyze
  • Videos with many comments take longer
  • Check your internet speed
