How to use WorthIt.AI

  1. Open the video in YouTube and tap Share.
  2. Choose WorthIt.AI from the share sheet.
  3. We fetch the transcript, real comments, and run our AI analysis.
  4. Read the summary, check the credibility signal, and ask follow-up questions.

Tip: Pin WorthIt.AI to your share favorites for faster access.

Fast answers

Why can analysis fail?

Most issues come from missing captions, age restrictions, or disabled comments. Try another video to confirm.

What if results are slow?

Long-form videos and thousands of comments take longer. Keep the app open; we notify you as soon as results land.

Is the score trustworthy?

The credibility signal blends transcript depth, sentiment, and historical patterns. Use it as a compass along with your judgment.

Troubleshooting checklist

  • Confirm your device is online (Wi-Fi or cellular).
  • Update to the latest WorthIt.AI version from the App Store.
  • Make sure the video has captions or auto captions enabled.
  • Restart the WorthIt.AI app if the share extension stalls.
  • Try sharing directly from the YouTube app, not the browser.
