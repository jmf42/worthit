We do not collect personal information such as name, email, or device identifiers. All data logged is used solely for diagnostics and performance improvement of the service.

Transcripts and summaries are cached for up to 24 hours for performance only, then deleted.

4. Analytics

We collect anonymous usage analytics to improve our service quality and reliability. This includes:

  • App launch events and feature usage
  • Video analysis success/failure rates
  • Cache hit/miss statistics
  • Network error patterns
  • Performance metrics (analysis time, etc.)

All analytics data is anonymous and does not contain personal information. We do not track individual users or create user profiles. Analytics data is used solely to improve service reliability and performance.

5. Processing with Artificial Intelligence

To generate summaries and analyses, we send the transcript and public comments to OpenAI. This data is used solely for AI processing and is deleted according to the timelines described above. OpenAI may store data in an aggregated or anonymized form to improve their models, but no personal user data is shared or retained by us.

6. Retention of Data

Transcripts and comments are cached for up to 24 hours. Server logs containing IP addresses, timestamps, and YouTube video IDs are retained for up to 30 days for operational diagnostics and security. This information is not linked to your identity.

7. Transfers to Third Countries

Transcripts and comments are processed in the United States via OpenAI servers. Although we do not send personal data, you acknowledge and consent to such transfer for service functionality.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under the age of 13. If we learn that a child under 13 has submitted personal data, we will delete it promptly.
