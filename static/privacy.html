Privacy Policy

Last updated: 31 May 2025

1. Who we are

WorthIt.ai is an educational tool that generates summaries and transcripts of publicly available YouTube videos. We do not represent or work for YouTube or Google.

2. Data we do not collect

  • No personal details (name, e-mail, Apple ID).
  • No tracking cookies or advertising identifiers.
  • No precise location data.

3. Data we briefly process

When you request a transcript the app sends the public video ID to our server. The server downloads the public captions, analyses them with OpenAI, and returns the summary. We discard the transcript and analysis after 24 hours for cache-performance only.

4. Analytics

Aggregate, anonymous usage counts (e.g. “how many summaries per day”) are stored to improve reliability. No user-level profiles are kept.

5. Contact

Questions? E-mail us at support@worthit.ai
