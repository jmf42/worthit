Privacy Policy

How we process data to analyze public YouTube content

1. Who we are

WorthIt.AI analyzes publicly available YouTube transcripts and comments to generate summaries, insights, and Q&A. We are not affiliated with YouTube or Google.

2. Data we do not collect

  • No personal profile (no name, e‑mail, or account creation)
  • No tracking cookies or advertising identifiers
  • No precise location data
  • No device identifiers or fingerprinting

3. Information we collect

  • When you request analysis, the app sends the public YouTube video ID (or URL) to our server to fetch transcripts and public comments.
  • Server logs may include IP address, timestamp, request path/parameters (including video ID), and basic headers purely for diagnostics and security.

We do not create user profiles and do not collect your name, email, or device identifiers.

4. Caching & retention

In‑memory caches: transcripts and comments are cached for ~2 hours to improve performance.

Persistent cache: we maintain a persistent on‑disk cache to speed up repeated requests; it does not have a fixed time‑based expiration and entries are overwritten/pruned during maintenance.

Logs: logs rotate by file size with rolling files; older entries are overwritten (not time‑based retention). None of this data is linked to your identity.

5. Analytics

We use app‑side logging and server logs (no third‑party analytics SDK) to measure reliability, such as:

  • Analysis success/failure rates and performance timing
  • Cache hit/miss statistics
  • Network error patterns

No advertising or cross‑site tracking.

6. AI processing

To generate summaries, insights, and Q&A, we send the transcript, a subset of public comments, and your questions to OpenAI’s API. We do not send personal information.

We may use public caption mirrors (e.g., Piped/Invidious) or tools like yt‑dlp and youtube‑comment‑downloader to retrieve public captions/comments when direct methods fail.

7. International transfers

AI processing may occur in the United States (OpenAI). We do not intentionally process personal data.

8. Your choices & requests

You can request information about logs or cached content associated with a specific video ID you submitted.

9. Children’s privacy

WorthIt.AI is not intended for children under 13. If we become aware of any personal data involving a child under 13, we will delete it.
