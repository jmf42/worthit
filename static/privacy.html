Privacy Policy

WorthIt.AI analyzes public YouTube content to deliver summaries and insights. We keep your personal data out of the loop.

1. Who we are

WorthIt.AI by TuliAI provides AI summaries, comment insights, and credibility signals for public YouTube videos. We are not affiliated with YouTube or Google.

2. Data we do not collect

  • No personal profiles, names, or emails.
  • No advertising identifiers or cross-site tracking.
  • No precise location data or device fingerprints.

3. Information we process

When you share a video, we receive the YouTube URL or ID plus optional prompt text. Server logs may capture IP address, timestamp, and headers for diagnostics.

4. Caching & retention

Transcripts and comment snapshots sit in memory for roughly two hours. We also maintain a persistent cache to accelerate repeat requests; entries are pruned during maintenance. Log files rotate by size.

5. Analytics

No third-party SDKs. We rely on internal metrics to monitor uptime, latency, and feature usage to keep the experience reliable.

6. AI processing

We send transcripts, selected public comments, and your follow-up questions to OpenAI’s API solely to generate WorthIt.AI responses. No personal data is included.

7. Data transfer

Processing may occur in the United States due to our infrastructure and OpenAI’s services.

8. Your choices

You can request removal of cached content or logs for specific video IDs you submitted by emailing worthit.tuliai@gmail.com.

9. Children’s privacy

WorthIt.AI is not intended for children under 13. We delete any personal data we inadvertently receive.

10. Updates

We will update this policy and adjust the date above when practices change. Continued use after changes means you acknowledge the update.
