Privacy policy

WorthIt.AI analyzes public YouTube content. We keep personal data out of the loop and only store what’s needed to keep things fast and reliable.

1. What we don’t collect

  • No personal profiles, names, or emails.
  • No advertising identifiers or cross-site tracking.
  • No precise location data or device fingerprints.

2. What powers WorthIt

When you share a link, we fetch the transcript, a subset of public comments, and send them to OpenAI strictly to generate summaries, scores, and answers.

3. Temporary caching

Transcripts and comment snapshots live in memory for roughly two hours to keep follow-up requests quick. Persistent cache entries get pruned during maintenance.

4. Diagnostics

Server logs may include IP address, user agent, and timestamps. We only use them for reliability and scaling decisions.

5. Third-party services

OpenAI processes summaries and Q&A. When transcripts are tricky to fetch, we may rely on public caption mirrors or tools like yt-dlp and youtube-comment-downloader.

6. Data residency

Processing may occur in the United States where our infrastructure and OpenAI services run.

7. Your choices

Email worthit.tuliai@gmail.com to request deletion of cached entries or diagnostic logs tied to specific YouTube IDs you submitted.

8. Children’s privacy

WorthIt.AI isn’t designed for children under 13. If we ever receive personal data from a child, we delete it.

9. Updates

We’ll update this policy when practices change. The “last updated” date above always reflects the current version.
